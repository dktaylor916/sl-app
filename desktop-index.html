Concentration of Waves Caught

How do the tides affect our surf?

We looked at over 11,000 succesful takeoffs from the past 3 years at Bantham to show you the best spot to be in the lineup. Shift the tide from high to low and rising to falling to see where the water lights up (concentration of caught waves). Zoom in to get a closer look at individual surf takeoffs, zoom out to get a more generalized view of the data.
